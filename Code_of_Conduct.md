# AIC-Quantum Code of Conduct

## 1. Ethical Primacy

Ethics precedes performance.
Safety precedes speed.
Human dignity precedes systems.

---

## 2. Zero Weaponization Policy

This project strictly prohibits:

- Development of quantum-enabled weapon systems
- Civilian cryptographic exploitation
- Infrastructure designed for population control

Violations result in immediate removal.

---

## 3. Transparency

- Architectural decisions must be documented.
- Major changes require written justification.
- No hidden intentions in commits.

---

## 4. Intellectual Humility

Quantum systems are complex.

Contributors must not:

- Claim absolute authority
- Use academic status as leverage
- Suppress dissent through hierarchy

---

## 5. Psychological Safety

- No personal attacks
- No manipulation
- No authority-based silencing

---

## 6. Escalation Process

Conflicts are resolved through:

1. Direct discussion
2. Community review
3. AIC Ethical Council mediation

---

## 7. Consequences

- Warning
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent removal
- Public transparency report (if necessary)

---

AIC-Quantum is not just technology.
It is a test of human maturity.
